How to Play

  • How to start: Choose a mode, then press Start.
  • Sequence rule: Watch the full sequence, then repeat the full sequence.
  • Practice mode: 3 lives, unlimited replays. On mistake you lose 1 life and retry the same level; at 0 lives, game over.
  • Normal mode: 0 lives, exactly 3 replays per game (counter shown). On mistake: game over.
  • Master mode: 0 lives, 0 replays, faster playback for extra challenge.
  • Replay rules: Replay shows the full current sequence; disabled during playback.
  • Checkpoints: Try to reach Levels 5, 10, 15, and 20 to earn a celebration. See how high you can get and aim for Level 100 for the ultimate celebration!
  • Switching modes: Changing modes restarts the game immediately (no refresh needed).
